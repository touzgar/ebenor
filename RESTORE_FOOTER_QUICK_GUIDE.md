# 🚀 QUICK GUIDE: Restore Footer Background Image

## ✨ EASIEST METHOD - Use Admin Panel Button

I've added a **"🔄 Restaurer l'image par défaut"** button for you!

### Steps:
1. Go to: **http://localhost:3000/admin/homepage/accueil**
2. Click on **📄 Footer** tab
3. Scroll to **🖼️ Image de Fond du Footer** section
4. Click the blue button: **🔄 Restaurer l'image par défaut**
5. Done! ✅

The original background will be restored instantly!
